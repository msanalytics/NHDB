Tips for use:

1. Hover on a county to filter data.
2.The Region filter allows you to view different portions of the country at one time.
3.The Provider State filter allows you to view one or more states at a time. To do this, click on the All box in the filter to first remove all states and then click on the state that you want to see. In order to zoom in to your selection, click the pin icon on the bar on the left side of the map
4. Click the dropdown in "Viewing Date" filter to view a particular date on the map. You can also click on the right arrow below the dropdown in order to see an animation from the earliest to the most current date.

Note: The County Confirmed Cases data has been normalized through the addition of County population data allowing the two to be compared against each other.
