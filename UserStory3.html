Nursing home resident and staff vaccination rates

Tips for use:
1. The top map shows resident vaccination rates by percentage for each nursing home. Hover over a dot to see information.
The bottome map shows healthcare personnel vaccination rates by percentage for each nursing home. Hover over a dot to see information.
2. Use the sliders to filter the nursing homes based on the percent of residents or staff vaccinated in the nursing home.
3. Use the Week Ending slider to see vaccination percentages up to the selected date.
4. The table below will show nursing homes and its vaccination percentages that result from the filters you apply.
5. The filters apply to both maps at the same time.
Data Source: CMS nursing home COVID-19 data
